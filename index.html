Our Core Programmes

We offer a range of specialized programmes designed to meet the demands of modern industry.

B.Eng in Mechanical Engineering

Our flagship undergraduate program, providing a robust foundation in core mechanical principles and design.

Postgraduate Studies (M.Eng / Ph.D.)

Advanced research and specialization opportunities in areas like thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and material science.
