About

About The Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri, is a center of excellence for learning, research, and innovation in the field of mechanical engineering.

Learn More

Mission

To produce world-class mechanical engineers who are well-grounded in theory and practice, and are capable of solving complex engineering problems.

Vision

To be a leading department of mechanical engineering in Africa, recognized for its transformative research and academic excellence.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Teamwork are the core values that guide our department in all its activities.
