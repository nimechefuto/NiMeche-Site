Our Academic Programmes

Discover the right path for your engineering career.

Undergraduate Programme (B.Eng)

Our Bachelor of Engineering programme is designed to provide a solid foundation in the principles of mechanical engineering, preparing students for successful careers in various industries.

Core Courses

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Solid Mechanics
  • Machine Design
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Heat Transfer

Specializations

  • Automotive Engineering
  • Industrial and Production Engineering
  • Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning

Postgraduate Programmes (M.Eng & PhD)

Our postgraduate programmes offer advanced study and research opportunities for students seeking to deepen their knowledge and contribute to the frontiers of mechanical engineering.

M.Eng Specializations

  • Advanced Fluid Mechanics
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Advanced Thermodynamics
  • Robotics and Automation

PhD Research Areas

  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Advanced Materials Science
  • Biomechanics
  • Sustainable Manufacturing
